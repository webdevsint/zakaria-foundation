Zakaria Foundation

Zakaria Foundation, a service organization, started its journey in Ishwardi, Pabna with a vow of human welfare. On Friday, January 3, 2025, the organization's temporary office was inaugurated by cutting the ribbon in Joynagar village of Salimpur Union of the upazila.

The names of the newly formed committee of 23 members of the organization were announced, including the foundation's president Golam Kabir, general secretary Enamul Haque, and others.

Since its inception, the organization has been providing scholarships to underprivileged and talented students, providing medical allowances to the sick, and distributing winter clothes to the needy and helpless.

Quran Studies

We support initiatives that provide accessible Quranic education, fostering spiritual growth and moral values within communities. Your contribution helps establish learning centers and provide resources for students of all ages.

Education

Investing in education is investing in the future. We provide scholarships, school supplies, and infrastructure support to ensure children have the opportunity to learn and thrive, breaking the cycle of poverty.

Medical Assistance

Access to healthcare is a fundamental right. We facilitate medical camps, provide essential medicines, and support treatments for those who cannot afford it, ensuring healthier communities.

Self Reliance Program

Empowering individuals to become self-sufficient is key to sustainable development. Our programs include vocational training, micro-financing, and skill-building workshops to create lasting economic independence.

Zakat

Fulfill your religious obligation with confidence. Your Zakat contributions are meticulously distributed to the most deserving, ensuring transparency and maximum impact on those in need.

Religious Institutions

We support the maintenance and development of mosques, madrasahs, and other religious centers, fostering environments for worship, learning, and community gathering.

Food Distribution

Addressing hunger is a priority. We organize regular food distribution drives, providing nutritious meals and food packages to families facing food insecurity, especially during crises.

Housing Assistance

A safe and stable home is foundational. We provide assistance for shelter, including repairs, rebuilding, and temporary housing solutions for displaced families and those in extreme poverty.

Natural Disaster

In times of crisis, we provide immediate relief and long-term recovery support to communities affected by natural disasters, offering humanitarian aid, shelter, and rebuilding efforts.

Youth Activities

Engaging and empowering the next generation is vital. We organize educational, recreational, and leadership development programs for youth, nurturing their potential and fostering positive community involvement.
